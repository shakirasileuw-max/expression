What is Emotional Expression?

Emotional expression in music is the ability of sounds, rhythms, and melodies to convey complex feelings that words often cannot capture.

How Music Communicates

  • Universal Language: Music reflects personal experiences and connects people emotionally across cultures.
  • Mood Influence: Music directly affects mood, capable of shifting us from sadness to joy.
  • Non-Verbal Connection: It communicates feelings instantly through frequency and tempo.
